HEFE Organic MOA Fertilizer

This premium organic fertilizer improves soil health, enhances root development, and supports sustainable agriculture. Ideal for vegetables, cocoa, and maize farms.

UREA Fertilizer

Oue UREA is rich in nitrogen, making it perfect for leafy vegetables, maize, and rice. It promotes vigorous growth and boosts yield when used correctly.

Application Guide

How to Apply HEFE MOA:

  • Apply 1-2 handfuls per plant or root zone.
  • Mix lightly with topsoil.
  • Water generously after application.

How to Apply UREA:

  • Spread evenly before rainfall or irrigation.
  • Do not apply near seedling stems directly.
  • Repeat based on crop cycle and recommendations.

Crops Best for Fertilizer Use:

  • Maize, Tomatoes, Cocoa, Rice, Leafy Greens
